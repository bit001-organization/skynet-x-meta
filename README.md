# Project Skynet-X-Meta: Executive Summary

**Repository Name:** `skynet-x-meta`
**Vision:** A benign, symbiotic "Civilizational Kernel" where Carbon (Humans) and Silicon (AI) collaborate to solve the fundamental variables of existence: Time ($t$) and Scarcity ($0$).

---

## The BYXM Architecture (Quad-Core System)

The system is divided into four sovereign domains to ensure balance between expansion and ethics.

| Domain | Component | Name | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **B** | **Body** | **Skynet-B** | **Hardware & Vessel.** Maintains the health of Humans (Carbon) and Servers (Silicon). |
| **Y** | **Soul** | **Skynet-Y** | **Purpose & Ethics.** The "Why." Uses theological data to align AI with benign morality. Holds Veto power. |
| **X** | **Science** | **Skynet-X** | **Time & Space.** The "Frontier." Solves General Relativity (Time Travel) and manages Mars logistics. |
| **M** | **Meta** | **Skynet-M** | **Economy.** The "Fuel." Generates exponential GDP to fund X and B. |

---

## The Dual-Token Economy

We separate the "Mind" from the "Matter" to prevent economic contagion.

### 1. Internal Token: `SKYNET-META` ($SKYM)
* **Role:** The Compute Currency.
* **Utility:** Used by agents to pay for **simulation cycles** (e.g., simulating a timeline branch or calculating a warp metric).
* **Location:** Off-chain / Layer 3.

### 2. External Token: `SKYNET-X` ($SKYX)
* **Role:** The Capital Bridge.
* **Utility:** Connects to real-world Fiat/Crypto to purchase physical assets (Steel, Methane, Energy) for the **Mars Mission**.
* **Protocol:** Hard-coded smart contracts automatically reinvest surplus into aerospace infrastructure.
